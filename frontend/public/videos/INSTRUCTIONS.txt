HOW TO ADD VIDEOS TO GALLERY
============================

1. Add your MP4 video files to this directory with these exact names:
   - community-service.mp4
   - cultural-event.mp4
   - educational-program.mp4
   - spiritual-gathering.mp4
   - featured-documentary.mp4

2. Video Requirements:
   - Format: MP4
   - Resolution: 720p or higher
   - File size: Under 50MB recommended
   - Include audio for full experience

3. The gallery will automatically detect and play these videos with full controls including:
   - Play/Pause
   - Volume control with sound
   - Fullscreen mode
   - Progress bar
   - Duration display

4. If videos are not available, placeholder content will be shown.

5. Poster images are already created in /images/ directory:
   - video-poster1.jpg (for community-service.mp4)
   - video-poster2.jpg (for cultural-event.mp4)
   - video-poster3.jpg (for educational-program.mp4)
   - video-poster4.jpg (for spiritual-gathering.mp4)
   - featured-video-poster.jpg (for featured-documentary.mp4)

Simply drop your MP4 files here and refresh the website to see them in the gallery!
